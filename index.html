Fei Jiang

My researches focus on both theoretical and applied statistics. My research areas include modeling, estimation, and hypothesis testing (sequential testing) in the Bayesian and frequentist settings. My earlier researches provide guidelines for the applications of the Bayesian approaches to clinical trial designs. My later researches place emphasis on using semi- or non-parametric methods for data analysis. The applications cover the areas of analyzing high dimensional data, functional data, survival data, and missing data.
