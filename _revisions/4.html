Fei Jiang

My interests are on both theoretical and applied statistics. I have experienced the modeling, estimation, and hypothesis testing (sequential testing) in the Bayesian and frequentist settings. My first project provides guidelines for the applications of the Bayesian decision-theoretic approach to the clinical trial designs. My later researches emphasize on increasing model flexibilities and robustness by using semi- or non-parametric methods. These techniques are widely applicable in the current statistical researches, such as in the fields of high dimensional data analysis, functional data analysis, survival analysis, etc.  On top of demonstrating the theoretical validity, I dedicate to investigate the practical applications of the statistical methods in each project. The extensive theoretical training helps me to capture the mathematical essentials of statistical problems. The  applications of the statistical methods enhance my sensitivity to the real world problems.
